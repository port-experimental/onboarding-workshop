## Your Learning Journey

### Phase 1: Understanding Port (30 minutes)
**What is Port and why does it matter?**

#### The Developer Portal Problem
Modern software development faces several challenges:
- **Tool Sprawl**: Developers use dozens of different tools and systems
- **Context Switching**: Constant switching between tools reduces productivity
- **Knowledge Silos**: Information scattered across multiple systems
- **Self-Service Bottlenecks**: Developers wait for ops teams for common tasks
- **Quality Inconsistency**: No unified view of service quality and standards

#### Port's Solution
Port provides a **unified developer portal** that:
- **Centralizes Information**: Single source of truth for all development resources
- **Enables Self-Service**: Developers can provision resources and trigger workflows themselves
- **Improves Visibility**: Real-time dashboards and metrics for better decision making
- **Enforces Standards**: Quality scorecards ensure consistency across teams
- **Reduces Friction**: Streamlined workflows and automated processes

### Phase 4: Implementation Strategy (30 minutes)
**How to successfully adopt Port**

#### Implementation Phases

**Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-2)**
- Set up Port instance and basic integrations
- Define initial blueprints for services and teams
- Import existing data from GitHub/CI systems
- Train platform team on Port administration

**Phase 2: Pilot (Month 2-3)**
- Roll out to 1-2 development teams
- Create team-specific dashboards and actions
- Gather feedback and iterate on configuration
- Measure initial impact and ROI

**Phase 3: Expansion (Month 3-6)**
- Scale to all development teams
- Add advanced integrations and workflows
- Implement quality scorecards and compliance tracking
- Establish governance and best practices

**Phase 4: Optimization (Month 6+)**
- Continuous improvement based on usage data
- Advanced automation and custom integrations
- Organization-wide standards and policies
- Regular review and optimization

#### Success Factors
- **Executive Sponsorship**: Clear support from leadership
- **Champion Network**: Enthusiastic early adopters in each team
- **Gradual Rollout**: Start small and expand based on success
- **Training & Support**: Adequate resources for user education
- **Measurement**: Track adoption and impact metrics

## Investment Overview

### Licensing Costs
- Port pricing is typically per-developer per-month
- Volume discounts available for larger organizations

### Implementation Costs
- **Platform Team Time**: 2-4 weeks for initial setup

- **Change Management**: Communication and adoption support

- **Administration**: 10-20% of one FTE for ongoing management
